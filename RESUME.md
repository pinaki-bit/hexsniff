### **HexSniff** | Enterprise Network Detection & Response (NDR) Platform
*Python, FastAPI, Scapy, React, Three.js (WebGL), SQLite, Docker*

- Engineered an enterprise-grade Network Intrusion Detection System (IDS) capable of capturing and analyzing live packet traffic at **1,800+ Packets/Sec** using Python, Scapy, and FastAPI.
- Defeated O(N²) time complexity bottlenecks during SYN Flood and DDoS attacks by architecting a custom **O(1) algorithmic connection tracker** and hash-map Threat Intel correlation engine.
- Implemented real-time streaming of parsed network telemetry via WebSockets to a custom React frontend, rendering live geographic traffic arcs in a **Three.js WebGL** 3D environment at a stable 60 FPS.
- Built a Forensic Validation Lab featuring offline PCAP parsing, automated **MITRE ATT&CK** coverage mapping, and an integrated **AI Analyst (Google Gemini)** to generate evidence-based incident reports.
- Engineered a SQLite **Case Management System** (WAL mode) for tracking indicators of compromise (IOCs), persisting threat evidence, and managing incident response workflows. 
- Containerized the entire architecture with **Docker Compose**, ensuring zero-setup portability for Linux forensic validation environments.
